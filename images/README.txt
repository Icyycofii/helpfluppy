==========================================
HOW TO ADD YOUR FLUPPY IMAGES
==========================================

Add these 3 images to this folder (public/images/):

1. fluppy.png - Fluppy's face (used in the maze)
   - Best size: 200x200 pixels
   - Square image works best
   - Can be PNG, JPG, or WEBP

2. fluppy-happy.png - Fluppy smiling/happy (used in celebrations)
   - Best size: 300x300 pixels
   - Show Fluppy looking happy!

3. fluppy-love.png - Fluppy with heart/cute expression (used in final reveal)
   - Best size: 300x300 pixels
   - The cutest photo of Fluppy!

TIPS:
- Remove background for best look (use remove.bg)
- Square images work best
- Keep file sizes under 500KB for fast loading

After adding images, refresh your browser!
